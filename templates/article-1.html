2021's Hottest Events
February 9, 2021

2021 kicked off hot for the Dream Team!

Topping off Molly and Jacob's month-long stay in Park City, the family joined the two of them slope-side for some down-hill shredding.

Flips, 360, and all around gnarly skiing. Below we recap key acheivements for each of our athletes.

Hannah:
Known as a catiously consistent skiier, Hannah showed that while slow and steady might not get you down the mountain first, it does allow you to tackle cooler mountains. After only one day on the mountain Hannah slammed a black diamond with moguls! Hungry for more, she spent days two and three crushing bumps and even dipped into a small wooded path.

Bec:
Rocking brand-new K2 Mind-Benders and the only flattering snow-pants we've ever seen, Bec took to the slopes with passion in her eyes. Flaunting new boots, she nailed turn after turn without a single fall, all while filming her possy of snowboarder bros. And it didn't go without notice that Bec stayed up past 10 every single night.
(disclaimer: Bec is officially sponsored by K2)

Tom & The Boys:
Making their first appearance at a Liebeskind vacation, Nick and Jack came to play! After just hours on a board, they launched their free-styling careers, popping S turns left and right and floating over jumps. Of course, we know their inspiration came from fearless leader and proud dad Tom who, after eventually getting up, effortlessly cruised down the slopes - proving to everyone that 8 years off won't hold him back.

Dad-Man Michael:
Grace is an understatement. Viewers everywher were amazed by the ease in which Michael slipped on his skis and after 2-years off showed no nerves as he glided down-hill. And equally impressively, throughout the trip Michael upgraded his gear - eventually almost blinding the competition with the contrast of his dark-shade goggles and rainbow face mask.

Molly & Jacob:
Trees, moguls, and jumps, nothing stops these two. Although the steepest hills come with fear and I-can't from Molly, Jacob's can-do attitude and at times stupidity, drove them to conquer and even love the blacks and double-blacks. You could see the joy in their faces at their opportunity to ski with their family and show off their new skillz.

Although the week was filled with big dinners, group game nights (shout out to Jack for winning hearts) and lots of love, there was one undeniable sadness.

"We love to be together. But it isn't the same without our big bro."
Said enough it basically became a moto - the event couldn't be perfect without an appearance from Dan, Danielle, and Lina. A trio that brings so much joy, fun, and inspiration to the rest of the group.

Up next:
The good news - the fun never stops with this Dream Team. Next stop, reuniting as a full crew at The Black Stallion , their 24-bedroom crib in The Outer Banks. March 8 - 21, we'll see you there.
