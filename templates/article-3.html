From Jingle Bells to Wedding Bells
February 14, 2021

Destiny, faith, aligned stars.

After years of cuddles, cuteness, and goofs these two love birds are tying the knot. Before they walk down aisle, we decided to take a look down memory lane...

B & T Take on the Farmers Market

Known for their love of food and cooking, B & T spend at least 4 hours each weekend at the farmers market.

Watching the Game at the Bar

As athletes themselves, it is only natural for B & T to spend their time hopping from bar-to-bar streaming football, basketball, and their most favorite, curling.

Traveling in Style

Big believers that the first class is the best class, B & T travel at high heights and in heigh fashion!

Fun in the Sun

While B & T love to enjoy their time in the sun, they always make sure Tom is fully protected.

Jingle Bells with the Girls

In addition to Tom, Bec brings a new winter holiday and these two cuties into the Dream Team.

Is it Destiny, faith, or aligned stars? It's hard to say for sure but one thing we do know, it's perfect!
