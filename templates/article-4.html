Conquering Distance One Topi at a Time
February 14, 2021

Described by the owners as "A more human video chat in a customized virtual world," topia.io transcends physical boundaries separating personal interaction with an interactive and customizable face-to-face video experience.

Born out of the need to bring people together amongst a global pandemic, topia allows users to replicate natural human interaction in the virtual world. Using keyboard arrows, users have the ability to move about the virtual space, entering and leaving conversations freely as they move closer and further away from others.

On top of the interaction element, Topia empowers users to build their own personalized world, leveraging both pre-built templates and custom art. With a tiered pricing system, users can invite up to 20, 100, or 10,000 people to interact within their personal environment.

This fabulous application is the cultivation of many years of hard work from Daniel and the continued efforts of his teammates. Of course, amongst that group is another Dream Teamer Danielle Diamond who leads up Initiatives and Business Strategy.

Already breaking grownd as a top-product in 2020, we expect to see Topia continuing to grow and change the way we virtually communicate!
